2. Unit Sequence

No. Unit Code Unit Title
1 NAT10809001 Apply Australian work health and safety practices in the electrical workplace
2 NAT10878001 Apply control measures for Australian refrigeration and air conditioning workplace hazards and risks
3 NAT10878002 Apply Australian standards to solve refrigeration and air conditioning low voltage circuit problems
4 NAT10878003 Disconnect and reconnect fixed wired electrical equipment connected to a low voltage supply
5 NAT10878004 Recover, pressure and leak test, evacuate and charge refrigerants
6 NAT10878005 Install refrigeration and air conditioning systems
7 NAT10878006 Diagnose and rectify faults in refrigeration and air conditioning systems and components
8 NAT10878007 Verify compliance and functionality of Australian refrigeration and air conditioning installations
9 NAT10878009 Solve problems in central plant air conditioning systems
10 NAT10809008 Source and purchase Australian material/parts for electrical work
11 NAT10878012 Install and commission Australian flammable refrigerant air conditioning and refrigeration systems
12 NAT10878013 Service Australian self-contained flammable refrigerant air conditioning and refrigeration systems
13 NAT10878014 Apply Australian safety procedures for handling flammable refrigerants

9. Portfolio Documentation - End of qualification

The End of Qualification Documentation is the final and essential stage of your qualification process.

At this stage, you are required to submit a complete set of documents to verify your work experience, licensing status, and compliance with regulatory requirements.

This documentation allows us to assess your eligibility for completion and certification.

Please ensure that all forms are completed accurately and that all supporting evidence is current and valid

All submitted documentation must be current and validated within the last three (3) months. Documents older than this period may not be accepted and may require re-issue or re-verification.

Document Requirement Details
Provisional ARC Licence Must be valid
Overseas Employer Reference Evidence of AC work experience
Australian Employer Reference(s) Minimum of 12 months experience
Referee Testimonials (x2) From licensed AC in Australia (form attached) Reference letters must include:
  • Company letterhead/logo and contact details
  • Employer name, signature, and license number
  • Duration of employment, role, and industry activities
  • Detailed scope of AC work undertaken
CPR Certificate HLTAID009 – issued within the last 12 months
Updated Resume Current and up to date
Employment History Form Form attached
Self-Assessment Questionnaire Form attached

Incomplete or outdated documentation may delay the processing of your qualification. If you have any questions or require clarification, please contact our administration team before submitting your documents.
