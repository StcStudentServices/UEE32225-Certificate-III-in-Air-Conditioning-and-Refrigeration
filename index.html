    Competency Policy

    To be marked as competent in a unit, you must successfully complete all of the requirements below.

    Think of competency as proving that you understand the work, can do the work, and have real experience doing it.

    Theory

    You must:

    • Complete all required theory assessments
    • Pass within a maximum of two attempts
    • Achieve a minimum score of 70%
    Practical

    You must:

    • Complete all practical tasks safely and correctly
    • Be assessed by a trainer

    Results will be:

    • Competent (C) ‚Äì you can do the task to the required standard
    • Not Yet Competent (NYC) ‚Äì you need more practice before passing
    Exemplar

    You must:

    • Record your workplace experience in Exemplar
    • Achieve at least 80% progress
    • Show both demonstration and experience progress
    Attendance

    Being present in class matters.

    You must attend at least:

    • 80% of classes for each unit

    If you are not in class, you cannot be assessed or supported properly.
